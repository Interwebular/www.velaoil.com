About

Vela Resources Corp. is a privately held helium exploration company focused on developing large resources of helium in southern Saskatchewan.

Vela is built on the foundation of a strong technical team and focused management.

Helium

Helium is an irreplaceable, and non-renewable commodity used extensively in high-tech industries like scientific research, manufacturing and healthcare. As those sectors continue to expand, the demand for helium follows.

Helium demand has increased at a 5% compounding annual growth rate and expected to grow at 8% through 2019.

The sedimentary basin of southern Saskatchewan has created an ideal reservoir to trap and accumulate this commodity of increasing demand.
